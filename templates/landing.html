What the tool actually does

Every run does the same thing: it pulls today’s top gainers and losers from the market, scores each stock from 1 to 10 (10 = strongest idea), and adds a short global review (sector, analysts, news) plus a “why enter now” line. You get up to 10 entry ideas per run — not hundreds of tickers, just a focused list with context.

  • Scans market movers (gainers & losers) from live data
  • Ranks each stock 1–10 so you focus on the best ideas first
  • Adds a global review (sector, analysts, news) per stock
  • Explains why enter now — momentum, volume, and context in one place
  • One-click Run now, CSV export, and links to Yahoo Finance for deeper research
